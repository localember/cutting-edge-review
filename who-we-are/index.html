Chiropractor
Dr. Richard Cutting, D.C.
Dr. Richard Cutting is the founder of Cutting Edge Chiropractic. After multiple surgeries related to sports injuries, he was told long-term pain medication might be part of his future.

Instead of accepting that as the only option, he began searching for a better path. That experience led him to chiropractic and still shapes the way he cares for patients today. Dr. Cutting earned his Bachelor of Science in Biology from Eastern Connecticut State University in 2009. He continued his education at Logan College of Chiropractic in St. Louis, where he earned a Bachelor of Life Sciences in 2010 and his Doctor of Chiropractic degree in 2012. He regularly pursues continuing education in areas including sports injuries, nutrition, extremity adjusting, pediatrics, and neurology. He takes a practical, patient-centered approach to care and offers a range of treatment options designed to help patients reduce pain, improve function, and stay active. In addition to chiropractic care, he also incorporates shockwave therapy, laser therapy, and acupuncture when appropriate to support each patient's goals. Dr. Cutting works with a wide range of patients, from athletes to busy parents to people who want to move better and feel better in everyday life. Outside the office, he values time with his wife and two kids.
Chiropractor
Dr. Matthew Chere, D.C.
Dr Matthew Chere has over 12 years of experience in Chiropractic care. He began his studies at Assumption University in Worcester, MA, earning a Bachelor's degree in 2001, he then obtained his Doctor of Chiropractic from the University of Bridgeport, College of Chiropractic in Bridgeport, CT.

A longtime Connecticut resident, Dr Chere worked at various practices before joining the team here at Cutting Edge. He is dedicated to building lasting relationships with his patients, ensuring that every treatment plan is personalized. Dr Chere provides effective, evidence-based care through diverse techniques and tools. He holds licenses in Certified EMG/NCV, Certified Diagnostic Ultrasound, Chiropractic Manipulative Therapy (CMT), and Therapeutic Rehabilitation Techniques. While fluent in English, he can also converse in Spanish.
